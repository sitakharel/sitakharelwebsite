My Projects

Embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of Bushwick in our documentary, a collaborative creation by Sita Kharel and partner Wakaba Oto for our Digital Technology class. Delving into the heart of a community undergoing transformation, our film explores the impact of gentrification through the lens of street art and urban works. Featuring candid interviews with fellow artists and residents, we unveil the intricate connection between artistry and the shifting landscape. From colorful murals to illicit graffiti, our subjects share their stories, shedding light on the evolving cultural fabric of Bushwick. This documentary not only captures the essence of the community but also serves as a testament to the power of art in preserving local narratives. Join us in celebrating the resilience of creativity amidst change, as we unravel the untold stories of Bushwick's streets.
